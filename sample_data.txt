# Antigravity AI - Company Overview

Antigravity AI is a leading artificial intelligence research and development company founded in 2024. Our mission is to build advanced, agentic AI coding assistants that empower developers worldwide.

## Key Products
1. **Nova Assistant**: A powerful, cross-platform IDE extension that provides real-time pair programming support.
2. **Quark Engine**: A specialized inference engine optimized for large-scale code analysis and transformation tasks.
3. **Orbit Cloud**: A managed platform for deploying and scaling AI-driven development workflows.

## Technical Stack
We primarily use Python for our research pipelines, while our product surfaces are built with TypeScript and React. Our vector store of choice is ChromaDB, which we use for efficient retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).

## Founder and Vision
The company was co-founded by Dr. Elena Vance and Marcus Chen. Their vision is a world where every developer has a personal AI collaborator that understands their codebase as well as they do.

## Office Locations
Our headquarters is located in San Francisco, with satellite offices in London, Tokyo, and Berlin.
